About Me

I’m a Data Analyst (MSBA, CSM) focused on building end-to-end analytics solutions—from ETL pipelines and data modeling to dashboarding and executive reporting. I design scalable data systems using star schema and semantic models to deliver real-time, reliable insights that support data-driven decision-making.

With expertise in SQL, KQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, I combine strong technical skills with Scrum-based program delivery to ensure projects are executed on time with clear metrics and measurable impact. I also excel at stakeholder collaboration, translating business needs into actionable analytics solutions across domains like healthcare and supply chain.
